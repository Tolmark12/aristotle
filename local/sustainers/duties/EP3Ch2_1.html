If you belong to the ISS group, you must assess each security patch within 35 days of discovery.

The system teams and asset owners must apply that patch or document a mitigation plan inside 35 days of assessment. This process is defined in the Security Patch Management Process. To mitigate patches, work with ISS to define, document and execute a mitigation plan.

POTENTIAL ISSUES

  • Not assessing patches for CRCAs within 35 days of release
  • Not applying patches to CRCAs within 35 days of assessment
  • Not documenting a mitigation plan within 35 of release
  • Not documenting inapplicability of unapplied patches
  • Not having documented progress on a mitigation plan
