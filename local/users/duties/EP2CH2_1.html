If you have access to a PSP, that means you have access to CRCAs attached to an ESP network. Never, never, never change the network cabling on assets.

TIP: Just don't touch assets. We know it's tempting.

POTENTIAL ISSUES

  • Attaching or removing assets on the ESP network without following the change control procedure.
  • Connecting CRCAs to different networks (e.g. plugging in a network cable, WiFi adapter or dial-in modem.)
