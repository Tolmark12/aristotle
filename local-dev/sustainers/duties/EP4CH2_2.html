If you have electronic access with a personal account, you must change your account passwords at least annually. Many systems will prompt you to change your password automatically when you try to log in. For systems that don't automatically prompt for the change,you will need to perform a manual password change. Please follow this guidance promptly.

POTENTIAL ISSUES

  • Not performing the recurring password-change tasks in a timely manner.
  • Not setting a password of the right complexity
  • Using "12345" as your password.
