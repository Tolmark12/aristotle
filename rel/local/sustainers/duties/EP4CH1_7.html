If you are responsible for configuring CRCAs, you may have responsibility to assign permissions in systems after the PRA approval process is completed. When giving a new user access to devices, handle this job with the utmost care. When removing someone's access, perform that removal in a timely manner - you only have 24 hours to remove access when an employee is terminated.

POTENTIAL ISSUES

  • Not disabling physical access to any PSP(s) within 24 hours of termination
  • Not disabling interactive remote access within 24 hours of termination
  • Not disabling access to BCSI locations by the end of the next calendar day following termination
  • Not revoking non-shared accounts within 30 days of termination
