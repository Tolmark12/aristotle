If you have individual user accounts on any CRCAs, you are not to share these accounts with other people. Ever. If you have a shared account, never share this account with other people. Ever.

This also includes PIN codes or any other personally identifiable information used for door controls systems or other access controls.

POTENTIAL ISSUES

  • Giving your personal account information to any other person.
  • Giving your shared account information to any person outside the documented users of that account
  • Willingly giving your password to a robot.
