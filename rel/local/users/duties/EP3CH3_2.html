Don't install/remove software without following the change control process. This may also seem obvious, but this can be as simple as bringing a corporate or personal laptop to a facility and transferring your MP3 collection to your workstation to listen to music while you work. Be vigilant.

If you need to get data or software onto your workstation or an asset, use the APS Software Installation Menu or if your selection is not on the installation menu, request it through Get it Here on Inside APS.

POTENTIAL ISSUES

  • Running or installing non-approved software on CRCAs (even if it doesn't require administrator permissions).
  • Transferring files to an ESP network through unapproved mechanisms.
